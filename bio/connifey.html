Tim Connifey is a senior security consultant at Vanguard. His focus is on the accounts in the greater Boston area. Connifey brings significant expertise as a security and logistics management expert with a deep understanding of travel and event security. He is a specialist in executive protection and crisis management.

More than 27 years of experience in law enforcement. Including time spent with the Attorney Generals Drug Task Force in an undercover capacity.

Assigned to and managed a security team of police officers at the 2002 Salt Lake City Winter Olympics. Liaison to NBC broadcasting at host venue and hotel to provide assistance with dignitary protection for musical artists and designated individuals.

Developed a security and travel strategy for oil industry executives during the 2007 Super Bowl in Scottsdale AZ. Worked with representatives of British Petroleum and Gulf Oil to guarantee safety and security of the clients.

Advisor to the Kelly Cares Foundation in matters of travel security concerning Coach Brian Kelly and his family during official Notre Dame Football events. Provided executive protection to Coach Kelly and family at events to include: the Kentucky Derby, NFL games, Kelly Gang events in NYC and various Kelly Cares Events throughout the U.S.

Facilitated travel logistics and security for J. Paul Reddam ownership group at the 2012 Belmont Stakes. To include executive protection and transportation of client and family.

Worked with Team/Ops security handling NFL level players. To include Kraft Group Charity events and Best Buddies charity event featuring NFL talent to include Tom Brady, Julian Edelman, Danny Amendola and others.

Specialist in logistics involving the transportation and security of multi-million dollar pharmaceutical shipments. Integral part of a team responsible for safe, secure delivery of product from international border to point of distribution.
